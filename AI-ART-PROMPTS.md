## GENERATION TIPS

1. **Use Midjourney or DALL-E 3** with "--style raw" for consistent pixel art
2. **Batch generate**: Request multiple frames in one image, then slice in Aseprite/Photoshop
3. **Resolution**: Generate at 2x-4x target size, then downscale for crisp pixels
4. **Transparent background**: Use "isolated on white" then remove background
5. **Color limit**: 16-32 colors per sprite for authentic pixel art feel
6. **Animation**: Generate key frames only, interpolate in-engine

## SUGGESTED AI TOOLS
- **Midjourney**: Best for stylized pixel art
- **DALL-E 3**: Good for specific prompts
- **PixelLab**: AI specifically for pixel art generation
- **Aseprite**: Manual pixel art creation (best quality)
- **GraphicsGale**: Free sprite animation tool
